# Admin User Manual

## Daily Workflow
1. Open Dashboard and check active drivers, pending leads, and coverage progress.
2. Open Route Planner to generate daily route sheets for all active drivers in the selected city.
3. Open Assignments and assign city > zone > district > streets to drivers.
4. Monitor Live Tracking during the day for offline, idle, skipped street, and route deviation alerts.
5. Review submitted leads in Leads Database.
6. Open each Lead Detail page, validate photos/GPS/contact info, then approve or reject.
7. Use Quality Control to check blurry photos, duplicate locations, required photo completion, and GPS match.
8. Export approved leads as Excel, CSV, PDF, WhatsApp summary, or client portal report.
9. At the end of the day, review Reports for driver performance, city coverage, and national KSA progress.

## Assignment Colors
- Red: Not assigned
- Blue: Assigned
- Yellow: In progress
- Green: Completed

## Lead Status
- New / Pending: Submitted by driver but not reviewed.
- Approved: Valid construction lead.
- Rejected: Not valid, duplicate, wrong phase, or poor evidence.
- Sent to Client: Delivered to client by export, WhatsApp, email, or portal.

## Quality Rules
- Billboard photo is mandatory.
- Front site photo is mandatory.
- GPS must be captured automatically.
- Photos should be watermarked with timestamp, GPS, driver name, and street.
- Duplicate leads should be reviewed before approval.
