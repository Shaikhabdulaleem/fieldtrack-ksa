# SECTION 1 — EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

---

## 1.1 Project Overview

```
ConstructTrack KSA is a Multi-City Field Data Collection 
and Lead Generation SaaS Web + Mobile Application designed 
for operations across multiple cities in the 
Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

The system enables MULTIPLE teams of field agents across 
MULTIPLE cities, each equipped with vehicles, GPS tracking 
devices, smartphones, and mobile internet SIM cards to 
systematically survey every zone, district, and street 
in their assigned city to identify and capture active 
construction sites in early phases.

The collected data is transformed into high-quality 
construction leads that are delivered to clients in 
real estate, construction, and related industries 
across KSA.

SCALE OF OPERATIONS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Cities Supported:     Multiple KSA Cities
Drivers per City:     Unlimited (Scalable)
Total System Drivers: Unlimited
Cities at Launch:     Starting with Jeddah
Expansion Ready:      Riyadh, Mecca, Madinah,
                      Dammam, Khobar, Tabuk,
                      Abha, Taif, Jizan & more
```

## 1.2 Problem Statement

```
CURRENT PROBLEMS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

FIELD OPERATIONS PROBLEMS:
❌ No system to track which streets drivers covered
❌ No way to verify drivers actually visited areas
❌ Cannot manage multiple cities simultaneously
❌ Cannot scale driver teams across cities
❌ No live visibility of driver locations
❌ Duplicate coverage of same streets
❌ Missed streets and entire districts
❌ No cross-city performance comparison

DATA COLLECTION PROBLEMS:
❌ Data collected on paper or Google Forms
❌ Photos not linked to GPS automatically
❌ No structured lead format
❌ Data inconsistency across cities
❌ No central database for all KSA leads
❌ Cannot filter leads by city or region

MANAGEMENT PROBLEMS:
❌ Cannot manage teams in different cities
❌ No accountability per driver per city
❌ No way to measure productivity by city
❌ Cannot assign city managers
❌ Manual reporting takes too long
❌ Client delivery is unstructured

BUSINESS GROWTH PROBLEMS:
❌ Cannot onboard new cities quickly
❌ Cannot add more drivers easily
❌ No system to bill clients per city
❌ Cannot track ROI per city operation
```

## 1.3 Proposed Solution

```
CONSTRUCTTRACK KSA SOLVES THIS BY:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```


## 7.3 Driver Assignment Module

```
FR-DA-001: City-Based Assignment
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
City Manager assigns drivers to:
Level 1: Entire Zone
Level 2: Specific Districts
Level 3: Specific Streets

FR-DA-002: Smart Distribution
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
System calculates auto-distribution:

INPUT:
- Number of drivers in city
- Total streets in city
- Working days available
- Working hours per day

OUTPUT:
- Streets per driver
- Estimated completion date
- Daily target per driver
- Suggested zone boundaries

EXAMPLE CALCULATION FOR JEDDAH:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Total Streets:           ~10,000
Number of Drivers:       6
Streets per Driver:      ~1,667
Streets per Day/Driver:  ~100
Working Days Needed:     ~17 days
Estimated Completion:    3-4 weeks

FR-DA-003: Assignment Rules
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- One street = One assigned driver
- No two drivers same street (no overlap)
- Driver cannot access streets outside assignment
- Unassigned streets shown in red on map
- Auto-reassign if driver is deactivated

FR-DA-004: Reassignment
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
City Manager can:
- Reassign streets from one driver to another
- Move entire zone between drivers
- Add new driver and redistribute workload
- Handle driver absence/sick leave
- System logs all reassignment history
```

## 7.4 Live GPS Tracking Module

```
FR-GPS-001: Real-Time Tracking
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
- GPS ping every 30 seconds from driver app
- Location stored in database with timestamp
- Live map shows all drivers in selected city
- Admin can switch between cities on map
- Each driver shown in different color
- Driver name shown on hover/click

FR-GPS-002: Tracking Data Captured
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Per GPS ping record:
- Driver ID
- City ID
- Latitude
- Longitude
- Speed (km/h)
- Accuracy (meters)
- Current Street ID (auto-detected)
```
